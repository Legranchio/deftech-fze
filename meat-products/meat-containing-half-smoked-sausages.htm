Meat and Meals

Meat containing half smoked sausages

We offer the variety of sausage products of our own production. These are the high-quality sausage products made according to individual recipes. All sausage products meet high quality standards and have a quality certificate and a veterinary certificate.

Now you can enjoy a delicious natural taste of halal sausages at a very nice price. We produce small sausages, wieners cooked, smoked, halfsmoked sausages, variety of prefabricated foods, and other delicacies.

We do not use liquid smoke and emulsions in our products. Our products are made using only natural spices.

We only use natural wood in the smoking process. The aroma of smoked products is achieved using the hard wood (oak, pear, apple, alder).
