MEAT PRODUCTS PRODUCTION

The meat production process takes place within modern plant, equipped according to the modern European standards, which is located in Ukraine. Plant produces around 1000 tons of deep frozen and chilled vacuum packed meat per month, more than 20 tons of meat product per day, and 1 million canned products per month, which promptly delivered to the retail sales places. We follow all the necessary hygiene and stringent technological requirements during the production, transportation, and preparation processes. The plant has complete production cycle.
