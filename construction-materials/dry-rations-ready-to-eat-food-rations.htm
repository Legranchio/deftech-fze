Dry Rations

Military standard, ready to eat food rations

Can be safely and promptly heated with a flameless MRE heater. Easy to read instructions. Produced GMO-free, from the best organic food producers of the “Bread Basket of Europe”. All ingredients are cleared for exports by sanitary and fitosanitary inspections, all necessary certificates are available. Variety of menu: different main courses (chicken and beef), side dishes. Various components, such as sweeteners, candies, crackers, honey, teabags, instant coffee sticks, jams , drinks can be added to the rations.

Packaging can withstand any rough handling, waterproof. Can be stored at the temperature range from -25 to +25ºC. Shelf life is no less than 3 years.
